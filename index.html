AI Enablement & Agentic Automation

I've built structured multi-agentic workflows using OpenClaw and Claude MCP — designing systems where AI agents call into business tools, route decisions, and operate with minimal human intervention. This includes launched web applications with AI integrations and automation pipelines connecting iPaaS platforms like n8n, Make, and Latenode to LLM-backed orchestration layers.

For operations and finance teams, the immediate value is in reporting acceleration, exception routing, and replacing manual decision overhead with reliable automated logic. Through NXU Cloud Solutions, AI enablement is an available consulting offering — from scoping realistic workflows to building and deploying agent-based tooling for your environment.
